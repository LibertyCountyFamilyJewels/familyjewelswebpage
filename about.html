Our Story

The Start

Founded in 2002 by Cyra Helifax, Family Jewels began with just a small studio, a handful of handcrafted designs, and a dream of making fine jewelry deeply personal.

The Establishment

As word spread, the first Family Jewels storefront opened. It became more than just a shop— it was a gathering place where stories were exchanged and milestones celebrated.

The Journey to Success

Over the years, Family Jewels has grown into a trusted name, blending classic craftsmanship with modern design. From engagements to heirlooms, our pieces continue to carry meaning across generations.
